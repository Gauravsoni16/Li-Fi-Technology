WORKING OF LI-FI

  • Li-Fi system mainly includes two parts namely the transmitter and receiver.
  • A photodiode is used to receive the LED flashes strengthens the signal & gives the output.
  • The input of the transmitter can be any kind of data like text, voice, etc
  • The receiver section includes photodiode as well as amplifier. Here, photodiode receives the LED bulb flashes then changes the flashes into electrical signals.
  • Finally, the amplifier receives the signals from the photodiode and amplifies to provide the output.
